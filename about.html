About Us

(feat. the 2022 Fightn' Engineers)

We love to Teach!

Any Skill Level. Any Major. No Application. Always.

Unlike many other teams, we have no application process, or requirements to join. We want everyone with an interest in robotics on our team, and put a lot of energy into creating a non-competitive environment.


In the 2023 season we onboarded 27 new members, most of which were freshman, and have been teaching them CAD, Machining parts, First Article Inspection, Linux, C++, Circuit Design, and so much more.

We Love to Compete!

A: Robot Paintball Laser-Tag

In the 2022 season as a rookie team, we formed an 'alliance team' with Purdue University and UT Ausitn to form PUTR, and together placed 4th overall at the North American Lone Star Championship, hosted by Texas A&M Univerity.


This year, with over a 600% increase in membership, we will be bringing our own set of 3 robots to not 1 but 2 competitions!

RMUL North America Regional Championship - 7/6/2023-7/9/2023 @ University of Washington
