About Us

(feat. the 2022 Thornbots)

We love to Teach!

Any Skill Level. Any Major. No Application Needed. Always.

The Rose-Hulman Thornbots are dedicated to an accessible and educational environment. We have no application process or requirements. With an emphasis on mentorship and collaboration, all a new member needs is an interest in the team and a passion for robotics.


In the 2023 season we were fortunate to onboard 27 new members. These members are already learning CAD, Machining and Fabrication, First Article Inspection, Linux, C++, Circuit Design, and more.

We Love to Compete!

In our rookie year of 2022, we placed 4th overall at the North American Lone Star Championship as an alliance with Purdue University and UT Austin.
