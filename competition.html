The Competition

What is Robomasters?

Robomaster is a robotics cometition started by DJI that had millions of viewers worldwide. Each year, teams design and build robots which fire eithr 17mm (Paintball size) or 42mm (Golfball size) projectiles at other robots in an arena. The ultimate objective of this being to destroy the opposing teams base, by hitting it's force sensitive armor panels with your projectiles.


In this Competition we do...

Robot Design

Machining

Computer Vision

Autonomous Movement

Supercapacitor Design

And much more!


To learn more, watch the Verge's video on the Robomaster competition.


The Robots of Robomasters

The Standard

(RHIT's 2022 Standard)

The Standard launches 17mm projectiles and is best at 1 on 1 combat with another robot.

The Sentry

(RHIT's 2022 Sentry)




The Sentry slides along a rail, optionally shooting projectiles, and providing an extra pool of health and imuinty to the base. To see how this was benifical in the 2022 competition, watch our match aginst VT with Purdue and UT Austin.

The Hero

(RHIT did not bring a hero in 2022, pictured is the DJI sample hero robot)

The Hero robot shoots 42mm projectiles, dealing the most damage of all the robots, especially toward bases. RHIT plans to bring our own Hero to competitions in the 2023 season.
